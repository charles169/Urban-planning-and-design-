Challenge: Building Sustainable Cities

Challenge Brief

Urban areas face increasing pressure from population growth and environmental concerns. Your challenge is to develop a plan or prototype for a sustainable city.

  • Integrate green transportation (e.g., cycling lanes, electric buses)
  • Design smart infrastructure that reduces energy use
  • Include green spaces and community areas
  • Ensure affordable and inclusive housing
